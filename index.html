Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical bathroom remodel take?

Most projects take 2-3 weeks from start to finish. Large or complex renovations may require 4-6 weeks. We provide detailed timelines during consultation.

What warranties do you offer?

We provide a comprehensive 5-year warranty on workmanship and lifetime warranties on most fixtures. All materials come with manufacturer warranties.

What is the typical cost range for a bathroom remodel?

Projects typically range from $15,000 to $50,000, depending on scope and materials. We offer flexible financing options and detailed quotes during consultation.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes, we are fully licensed, bonded, and insured. Our license number is available upon request, and we maintain $2M in liability insurance.

Do you handle permits and inspections?

Yes, we manage all necessary permits and scheduling of inspections. Our team ensures compliance with local building codes and regulations.
